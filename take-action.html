TAKE ACTION

Join the Lorax and millions of people as they fight for the environment!

What you can do:

Optimize energy use

Use energy efficient lightbulbs like LED's, hang dry clothes, unplug electronics, lower your thermostat in the winter and raise it in the summer, etc

Use reusable/environmentally friendly products

Use reusable water bottles, bags, buy sustainable foods, clothing, etc. If you're using disposables, make sure to recycle and compost.

Go on marches and strikes!

Find out about climate strikes and marches happening near you!

Checklist:

Once you have completed an item, check it off your list!

  • Use LED lightbulbs
  • Unplug electronics
  • Change your thermostat
  • Use a reusable water bottle
  • Use reusable bags
  • Go on a climate march!
